Welcome Message

Inspiring Excellence Since 2002

The journey of Salahuddin Ahmed School and College began with the vision of nurturing students through quality education and human values. We are committed to building a strong foundation of excellence through modern technology-based learning, the close supervision of skilled educators, and a supportive environment. Every student is equally important to us, and our primary focus lies in the development of their merit and creativity.

We believe that this institution will flourish as a 'shining center for grooming future ideal citizens,' which is the dream of our founder, former Member of Parliament, Mr. Alhaj Salahuddin Ahmed. To turn this dream into reality, the integrated and responsible cooperation of students, teachers, and guardians is essential. May Allah help us.

Munshi Md. Nurul Alam

Principal
