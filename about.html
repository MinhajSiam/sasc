About Us

Know more about our history, mission, and vision.

Our Mission

To provide a safe learning environment that encourages academic excellence, creativity, and moral integrity. We aim to empower students with the skills necessary to thrive in a global society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Bangladesh, recognized for producing enlightened individuals who contribute positively to the nation and the world.

Our History

A Legacy of 28 Years

Established in 1995, Salahuddin Ahmed School and College started with a humble beginning of only 50 students and 5 teachers. Over the decades, we have grown into a premier institution with over 2,500 students.

Our journey has been marked by consistent academic success, winning National Awards in 2010 and 2018. We continue to upgrade our facilities to meet modern educational standards.
